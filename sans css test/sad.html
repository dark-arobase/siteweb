About ShineTime Car Wash

Since 2010, ShineTime has been the premier destination for car washing and detailing services in the region. We take pride in delivering exceptional quality and service to every vehicle that comes through our doors.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology to ensure that your vehicle receives the best care possible. From basic washes to premium detailing, we offer a range of services to meet your needs and budget.

At ShineTime, we believe that a clean car is a happy car, and a happy car means a happy owner. That's why we go above and beyond to ensure that every vehicle leaves our facility looking its absolute best.

Premium Products

We use high-quality, pH-balanced products that are safe for your vehicle's paint and finish.

Quick Service

Our efficient process ensures you get back on the road with a clean vehicle in no time.

Eco-Friendly

We use environmentally friendly products and water reclamation systems to minimize our environmental impact.

Experienced Team

Our trained professionals have years of experience in providing exceptional car care.
